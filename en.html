Biography

I am currently a master's student in the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science at Gannan Normal University. My research interests include non-parametric machine learning, interpretable granular-ball computing, clustering, classification, spectral clustering, and minimum description length based model selection. My current work focuses on adaptively characterizing local data structures with reduced manual parameter intervention, while improving the interpretability and robustness of model generation and decision-making processes.
